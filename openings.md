<ul class="content-list">

</ul>

<h2>Opportunities</h2>
<ul class="content-list">

  <li><strong>Master students / 硕士生:</strong> Students interested in micro-robotics, magnetic actuation, fabrication, control, or biomedical applications may inquire about available opportunities.</li>
  <li><strong>Postdoctoral associates / 博士后:</strong> Candidates with research experience in microrobotics, medical robotics, magnetic actuation, intelligent control, or bioengineering are encouraged to get in touch.</li>
  <li><strong>Research assistants / 科研助理:</strong> Candidates with experimental, fabrication, programming, imaging, or system integration experience may contact the lab for potential openings.</li>
